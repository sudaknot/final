Discover the Beauty of Taniti

Taniti offers a blend of adventure and serenity, making it an ideal destination for every traveler. The island's diverse landscapes include sun-kissed beaches perfect for lounging and snorkeling, as well as lush rainforests filled with exotic wildlife and scenic hiking trails. The active volcano stands as a breathtaking centerpiece, providing awe-inspiring views and thrilling excursions. Beyond natural wonders, Taniti boasts a rich cultural heritage, showcased in its traditional crafts, music, and local history museum. Visitors can also enjoy unique activities such as chartered fishing tours, helicopter rides, and vibrant nightlife at the new dance club and microbrewery in Merriton Landing. Whether you're seeking adrenaline-pumping adventures or peaceful retreats, Taniti promises an unforgettable experience. Explore the myriad attractions that make this island a true paradise.

Sights to See!

Taniti City

This city features native architecture and nearby white, sandy beaches that encircle Yellow Leaf Bay.

Hiking Spots!

Explore Taniti's stunning landscapes on our well-marked hiking trails. From easy walks to challenging treks, each trail promises breathtaking views and encounters with exotic wildlife. Most popular is Taniti's active volcano!

Bus Tours

Delve into the rich history and culture of Taniti at our local museum. With fascinating exhibits and artifacts, you'll gain a deeper understanding of the island's heritage and traditions.

Boat Tours

Discover Taniti's picturesque coastline and hidden coves on a guided boat tour. Enjoy the clear blue waters and abundant marine life as you sail around the island's scenic shores.

Entertainment

Taniti History Museum

Dive into Taniti's rich past at the History Museum, where fascinating exhibits bring the island's heritage to life.

Snorkeling

Discover the vibrant underwater world of Taniti with our guided snorkeling tours.

Helicoptor Rides

Take in breathtaking aerial views of Taniti's diverse landscapes with an unforgettable helicopter ride.

Night Life

Enjoy Taniti's vibrant nightlife with options like movie theaters, pubs, a microbrewery, bowling, a dance club, and an arcade.

Ziplining

Experience the thrill of soaring through the rainforest canopy on our exhilarating zipline courses.

Art Galleries

Explore the creative spirit of Taniti through its numerous art galleries, showcasing local and international talent.
