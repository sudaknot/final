Transportation
Effortless Travel Around Taniti Island

Navigating Taniti is a breeze with its comprehensive and convenient transportation options. The island is served by a small airport that accommodates both small jets and propeller planes, with plans for expansion to welcome larger jets in the near future. For ground transportation, Taniti City offers a reliable public bus system that operates daily from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m., while private buses serve the rest of the island. Taxis and rental cars are available from a local agency near the airport, ensuring easy access to all major attractions. For those who prefer a more active mode of transport, bicycles and helmets can be rented from various vendors, perfect for exploring the flat and walkable areas of Taniti City and Merriton Landing. Additionally, a cruise ship docks at Yellow Leaf Bay once a week. Whether you're planning to stay close to the city or venture out to the island's scenic spots, Taniti's transportation network makes it easy to get around and enjoy everything the island has to offer.
